About Our School

Founder of Maharishi School of the Age of Enlightenment, who introduced the Transcendental Meditation program to the world 50 years ago for systematically developing higher states of consciousness, Maharishi is now offering every school consciousness-based education so that all students everywhere can enliven total brain functioning, unfold their full creative potential to enjoy healthy, happy and successful lives.

Transcendental Meditation and T.M. Sidhi Programme draw their inspiration from Maharishiji's Master. His Divinity Swami Brahmananda Saraswathi Maharaj, who was the Shankaracharya of Jyothirmath, in Northern India. Maharishiji and his Master came from a tradition whose roots go back to the beginning of time and which includes great saints such as Vashista and Vyasa, who saw within themselves the delicate impulses of pure knowledge, the Veda, which are the very basis of life. The precious knowledge, which has made Transcendental Meditation so effective and successful and has made it possible for us to become Sidhas, comes from this glorious tradition.

School Biography

MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPAL

Jai Guru Dev !
“The secret of success is conviction and persistence.” – Maharishi Mahesh Yogi
Maharishi Vidya Mandir Metric Higher Secondary School , Ayanavaram is affiliated to the School Education Department, Government of Tamil Nadu and has had its inception in the year 2015.It is a branch school of Maharishi Vidya Mandir Metric Higher Secondary School, Ayanavaram ,Chennai. Our Founder is His Holiness Maharishi Mahesh Yogi, one of the foremost teachers of Transcendental Meditation which is followed world over by more than 10 million people. Our School Motto is “Knowledge is Structured in Consciousness” and we strive to help students unfold their fullest potential, through “Consciousness” based learning. The School is located at No. 78, Solai Street, Ayanavaram,Chennai-23. It is a co-educational English medium School following play way methodology for Kindergarten classes and the State Board Curriculum for classes PreKG to XII. Our curriculum has been carefully designed to foster intelluctual curiosity,creativity and critical thinking skills. Teachers try to transmit knowledge and contents to students according to their age,capabilities,skills and their potential. Teachers and Staff are trained to provide a supportive environment where children feel safe and secure. Teachers continuously equipping themselves by attending Teachers Training programmes and other training to update their knowledge to compete with 21 st century skills and Teaching Learning process and to give Experiential Learning. Apart from the curriculum , the school organizes a number of extra-curricular and co-curricular activities.This is to develop high levels of competency and participation in Intra- school and Inter- school Competitions. Students are exposed to a variety of academic programmes like Olympiads, Spell Bee, NTSE, All India quizzing Programmes, Culturals to develop their innate talent and boost up their levels of confidence when young.
